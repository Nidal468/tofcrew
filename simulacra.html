Simulacra are what you get from Standard and Limited Banner pull. There is 3 things that you get when you obtain a Simulacra. First is the weapon which you can use whenever you want then there is the skin which doesn't give any spacial attributes but it does make you look cool and last but not least there is the trait feature which will give you a spacial ability after reaching a certain friendship point with that Simulacra.

There are 4 different elements with an extra 5th element which we will explain later.

FROST UNIT

Frost units are those Simulacra who are specialized in Frost element and some of them have Ice Resonance which you will learn later in Resonance Guide.

SSR UNIT

FLAME UNIT

FLAME units are those Simulacra who are specialized in Flame element and some of them have Flame Resonance which you will learn later in Resonance Guide.

SSR UNIT

VOLT UNIT

Volt units are those Simulacra who are specialized in Volt element and some of them have Volt Resonance which you will learn later in Resonance Guide.

SSR UNIT

PHYSICAL UNIT

Physical units are those Simulacra who are specialized in Physical element and some of them have Physical Resonance which you will later learn in Resonance Guide.

SSR UNIT

ABERRATION UNIT

Aberration units are those Simulacra who are specialized in Aberration element and they have a unique type of Resonance which you will later learn in Resonance Guide.
